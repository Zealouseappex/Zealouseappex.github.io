MORE ABOUT BLAKE BUCZKIEWICZ


A young, energetic actor with a natural sense of his presence and that of the camera, Blake Buczkiewicz has taken on the Chicago film scene, completing over a dozen films at the start of his career. While just learning to read, his unique ability to memorize lines, keen acceptance of direction and adoring vigor have earned him roles in webisodic series, television pilots and on stage. With modeling and commercials also under his belt, Blake is ready for the next challenge.
